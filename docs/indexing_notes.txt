INDEXING NOTES
The purpose of the index is to give the reader an informative, balanced portrait of what is in the book and a concise,
useful guide to all pertinent facts in the book.

These facts, in the form of an alphabetically ordered list of main entries
and subentries, will include both proper names and subjects.
